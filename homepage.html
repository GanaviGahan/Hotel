Single rooms

A Single Room is designed for solo travelers, offering a cozy and comfortable space with a single or twin bed. These rooms typically range from 150 to 200 square feet and come equipped with essential amenities such as free Wi-Fi, a television with cable, a work desk, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with a shower. Guests can also enjoy complimentary toiletries, a tea/coffee maker, and room service for added convenience.

Double Room

A Double Room is ideal for two guests, featuring either a double or queen-size bed, or two twin beds for those who prefer separate sleeping arrangements. With a slightly larger space of around 200 to 300 square feet, these rooms include modern comforts such as free Wi-Fi, a TV, a mini fridge, a wardrobe, and a well-appointed private bathroom with a shower or bathtub. Air conditioning ensures a pleasant stay, while room service and complimentary toiletries enhance the overall experience.

Suite Room

the Suite Room offers an elegant stay with separate living and sleeping areas, making it perfect for couples or families. Spanning between 400 and 800 square feet, suites come furnished with a king-size bed, a spacious seating area, and optional sofa beds for additional guests. High-end amenities such as a large TV with premium channels, a mini bar, a coffee machine, and a private bathroom with luxury toiletries, a bathtub, and a shower provide a premium experience. Some suites also feature a balcony, offering scenic views.
